      The great school of Magwar model was established in the year 2016
      under the economic stimulus program in conjunction with the county government
      The program was proposed by the great professor in the Nyanza region, Peter Anyang' Nyong'o
      The school is the only centre of Excellence in the whole of Kisumu county
      Magwar model boys high school is one of the extra-county schools in the county
      which are required to have developed highly even to the national level by vision 2030
      and is expected to admit students from across the nation as the county itself is cross-mopolitan
      The school is located in the county of kisumu along holo-magwar road and is approximately 30km from Maseno

      The general structure and outlook of school and students

      Magwar model school id two-streamed and there are plans
      to make it a four-streamed school by 2030 due to increasing
      enrolment per year which is also as a result of the pleasing perfomance
      The dressing code of the school comprises of a biege pair of trousers,
      a white short-sleeved shirt,a biege blazer,black leather shoes,grey socks and a maroon tie
      The school has got six houses accomodating a total of 600 students in the boarding section.
      Magwar model boys high school has one administration block,
      three laboratories all funded by the government The school has a single computer laboratories but there are plans to construct more
      to eliminate the congestion by the students taking computer studies especially during practicals.
      The school offers a vast of subjects among them foreign languages such as French
      to allow the students interact freely with limited problems with fellow students from across the globe
      The school is under Straiht security services who maintain security in the school 24 hours a day
      The school accomodates staff members who can not commute and

      The school has planted numerous numer of trees which has given it a green cool
